Section 1: Overview

Originator:Office of the Minnesota Secretary of State

Title: Minnesota General Election Results, 2012-2018

Abstract: This file contains election results from Minnesota state general elections that took place following the 2012 redistricting. The results are at the voting precinct level, and include all federal races, all state races (except judicial races), and constitutional amendments. For US President, US Senator, and Minnesota constitutional office races, results are provided for all filed candidates. For other races, results are provided for all major party candidates. Voting precinct boundaries are specific to each election.

Purpose: This dataset combines the geographic database of voting precincts for the State of Minnesota with official results for even year general elections for display and analysis.

Time Period of Content Date: 12/17/2018

Currentness Reference: Date the most recent election was canvassed by the State Canvassing Board. 3/15/19: voting equipment updated for Brown, Cook, Koochiching, Red Lake, Rock, Roseau, Steele, and Wadena counties.

Progress: Complete

Section 2: Data Quality

Attribute Accuracy: Precinct and district attributes from Statewide Voter Registration System (SVRS). Election results certified by State Canvassing Boards. Election statistics certified by County Canvassing Boards.

Logical Consistency: Topology checks run to try to eliminate gaps and overlaps betweeen polygons.

Completeness: Includes results of all federal races, state-level non-judicial races, and constitutional amendments, by voting precinct. For US President and Governor races, includes all filed candidates. For other constitutional officers, includes all major and minor party candidates. To find results for races not included in this data, go to http://www.sos.state.mn.us/elections-voting/election-results/

Horizontal Positional Accuracy: The positional accuracy of the precinct boundaries is generally the same as the sources the precinct datasets were derived from--see https://gisdata.mn.gov/dataset/society-redistricting-2010 and https://gisdata.mn.gov/dataset/bdry-mn-city-township-unorg

Vertical Positional Accuracy: Not applicable

Lineage: 2012 boundaries derived from precinct data from Hennepin and Dakota Counties and U.S. Census blocks from 2010 redistricting data. May not include annexations and detachments after January 1, 2010.

2014, 2016, and 2018 boundaries originally derived from Minnesota's City, Township, and Unorganized Territory (CTU) Boundaries dataset (2013) and U.S. Census redistricting data. Boundaries incorporate election district boundary corrections made under MS 2.91, subd. 2 and MS 204B.146, subd. 3, and municipal boundary adjustments that were effective prior to each election, as well as improvements in boundary accuracy found in official sources.

Precinct and district attributes from Statewide Voter Registration System (SVRS). Election results certified by State Canvassing Boards. Election statistics certified by County Canvassing Boards.
